# Dental clinic

## Schedule

The clinic is open Monday to Saturday from 09:00 to 21:00.
Sunday is closed.

## Services and prices

- Therapist consultation: 30 minutes, from 100,000 UZS.
- Professional cleaning: 60 minutes, from 350,000 UZS.
- Dental treatment: 90 minutes, price depends on examination.
- Surgery appointment and extraction: price depends on surgeon examination.

## Doctors

The clinic has a dental therapist and a dental surgeon.

## Address

The clinic address will be clarified by the administrator during booking.

## Contacts

Leave your phone number in the Telegram bot so an administrator can contact you.

## Booking

The bot can help book a consultation, cleaning, or treatment.
For pain, symptom questions, or unclear situations, the bot offers a consultation but does not provide medical advice.
